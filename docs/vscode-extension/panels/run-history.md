# Run History Panel

The Run History panel shows the history of your Bruin runs, so you can review past executions without leaving VS Code. It's located at the bottom of the interface, near the other Bruin panels.

## Functionality

- **Review past runs**: See a list of your recent runs with their status and timing.
- **Grouped backfills**: Local backfill runs are grouped as expandable rows, so all the chunks of a single backfill stay together.
- **Quick reference**: Check back on what you ran and how it turned out.

## How to Use

1. Open the Run History panel from the bottom panel area.
2. Browse the list of runs to see their status and timing.
3. Expand a backfill row to see its individual chunk runs.
